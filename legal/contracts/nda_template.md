## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to enable the Disclosing Party to share certain confidential, proprietary, or sensitive information related to the Seed project (“Confidential Information”) with the Receiving Party for the purpose of evaluation, collaboration, research, mentorship, investment consideration, or technical review (the “Purpose”).

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

“Confidential Information” includes, but is not limited to:

- Technical designs, system architecture, and protocols
- Hardware specifications and device designs
- Software source code, algorithms, and data structures
- Business plans, market research, and financial models
- Investor materials, pitch decks, and strategy documents
- Research findings, simulations, and prototypes
- Any non-public information disclosed orally, visually, or in writing

Confidential Information may be marked as confidential but does not need to be explicitly labeled to be protected.

---

## 3. Exclusions from Confidential Information

Confidential Information does **not** include information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available without breach of this Agreement
- Was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the Confidential Information
- Is lawfully obtained from a third party without restriction

---

## 4. Obligations of the Receiving Party

The Receiving Party agrees to:

- Use Confidential Information solely for the stated Purpose
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
- Take reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality of the information
- Restrict access to Confidential Information to individuals who need it for the Purpose
- Promptly notify the Disclosing Party of any unauthorized disclosure

---

## 5. No License or Ownership Transfer

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any rights, licenses, or ownership interest in the Confidential Information, intellectual property, or related materials of the Disclosing Party.

All rights remain exclusively with the Disclosing Party.

---

## 6. Return or Destruction of Materials

Upon request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly:

- Return all Confidential Information, or
- Destroy all copies (physical or digital)

and confirm such destruction in writing if requested.

---

## 7. Term and Survival

- This Agreement begins on the Effective Date.
- Confidentiality obligations shall survive for **5 years** after the termination of discussions, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
- Trade secrets remain protected for as long as they qualify as trade secrets under applicable law.

---

## 8. No Obligation to Proceed

This Agreement does not obligate either Party to enter into any further agreement, partnership, or transaction.

---

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction where Seed is incorporated (or intended to be incorporated), without regard to conflict-of-law principles.

---

## 10. Remedies

The Receiving Party acknowledges that unauthorized disclosure may cause irreparable harm. The Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies available at law or equity.

---

## 11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning confidentiality and supersedes all prior discussions or agreements relating to the subject matter.

Any amendments must be made in writing and signed by both Parties.
